EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - SAMPLE CONTENT

LEAVE POLICY
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year, accrued monthly. Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Sick leave is provided separately, with 10 days per year.

WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible working arrangements are available upon manager approval. Core hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

WORKPLACE ISSUES
Any workplace issues should be reported immediately to your direct supervisor or HR representative. For confidential matters, employees can use the anonymous reporting hotline at 1-800-REPORT. All reports will be investigated thoroughly while maintaining confidentiality.

BENEFITS
Health insurance coverage begins on the first day of employment. The company covers 80% of the premium for employees and 60% for dependents. Additional benefits include:
- 401(k) with 5% company match
- Dental and vision insurance
- Life insurance
- Annual wellness allowance of $500

DRESS CODE
Business casual attire is expected in the office. Jeans are permitted on Fridays. Client meetings may require formal business attire.
